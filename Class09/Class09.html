Q1: What percentage of structures in the PDB are solved by X-Ray and Electron Microscopy.

n.total <- sum(stats$Total)
n.xray <- sum(stats$`X-ray`)
n.EM <- sum(stats$EM)

#Use the round function to round to 2 significant figures
round((n.xray/n.total)*100, 2)
[1] 81.48
round((n.EM/n.total)*100, 2)
[1] 12.22

Therefore, 81.48% of the total structures are solved by X-ray; 12.22% of the total structures are solved by EM.

Q2: What proportion of structures in the PDB are protein?

protein_only <- stats[stats$`Molecular Type` == "Protein (only)", ]
p.total <- protein_only$Total
round((p.total/n.total)*100, 2)
[1] 86.05
protein <- stats[stats$`Molecular Type` == "Protein (only)" | 
                   stats$`Molecular Type`=="Protein/Oligosaccharide" |
                   stats$`Molecular Type`=="Protein/NA", ]
round((sum(protein$Total)/n.total)*100, 2)
[1] 97.92

Therefore, 86.05% of the total structures are protein only; 97.62% are protein only or protein combined with nucleic acids/oligosaccharide.
